Naoki Yoshida (Yoshi-P)

Singlehandedly saved Final Fantasy XIV

Important contributions to a dying game:

  • 2010 - Became producer for the first time.
  • He had to become acquainted very fast to gain the trust of his development team.
  • Yoshida took the advice from his team to make sense of what the game was lacking.
  • He was able to turn everything around and made a failed game into a very successful one.
  • With hard work, dedication, and belief in himself, he was able to accomplish alot in his career.

"Speaking of Final Fantasy 14, I would like to restore that part a little bit. If we do that, we can give everyone a better challenge, in a good way, then ever before."

-Naoki Yoshida
